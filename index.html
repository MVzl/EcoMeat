Our Objective

Make our meat imitation accessible to everyone so they can have the nutrients meat contains for a much lower price. This is based on the UN Goal for Sustainable Development number 2: Zero Hunger. It consists of ensuring access to healthy and safe food to every person on the planet.

How is it made?

We will be using the Washed Flour Method, which consists of "washing" the dough in the water. Doing this we leave only the gluten, the protein of flour. Then we add the vegetables and legumes that have all the wonderful nutrients. Once the dough is throughly mixed, it is simmered in broth for 2 hours. EcoMeat is now ready!
